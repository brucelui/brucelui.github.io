My name is Bruce

and I am an inspiring designer whom wishes to make compelling designs to improve and bring value to people's lives.

It all started out...

back in High School where I discovered that I really enjoyed helping others, meeting new people and seeing new things through volunteering services. This made me want to do something meaningful as a career, but at the same time I couldn't let go off my passion for digital art and technology.

That's when I started going to school here.

SFU's School of Interactive Arts and Technology helped me discover about UCD (User Centered Design). Something that I can combine my love for digital technology and designing them with people in mind.

I love what storytelling does to people.

I don't think I can write a novel, but I enjoy taking photos that can tell a story. Not only does it help me look at the things around me at a different angle, but it evokes people's emotions and brings meaning to the table. This helps me practice empathizing and question potential problems.


I took every photo in this portfolio, check out more of my photos in my 500px page!

I'm still trying to learning as much as I can

and I will never stop. I cherish every opportunity I get to learn whatever I can. There are a lot of things that I want to go out and discover in the world. Life's a great big exciting adventure.
If you read up here and expected something interesting, I'm sorry. I just wanted to catch your attention through content design. Thanks for reading!

- Bruce Lui
